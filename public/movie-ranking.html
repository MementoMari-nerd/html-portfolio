The Princess bride

Monty Python and The Holy Grail

This movie is a cult classic for a reasion. From the incredible tone and jokes of the movie.

Stardust

A lovely movie following the story of two people. One of the main villains being played by Robert De Niro. I loved this movie as a kid and used to watch it multiple times while growing up.
